rules

in basketball to win your team must have higher points than other team.

for the game to start a refree has to toss a ball into the air while centers have to time the jump to tip the ball to their side of the court. this is either called tip off or jump ball

1.foul: a foul happens when another player makes illegal contact with another player. there are different fouls such a offensive,personal,flagrant,loose ball and technichal

flagrant is when a person intentionally commits a foul. technichal is when player disrespects a official or another player. offensive is foul commited on offence. loose ball foul is when foul is commited to player who isnt in possesion of the ball. and personal is normal foul

2.violations: if you violate rules in basketball you will lose possesion of the ball and other team will gain it

there are a lot of violations i will start naming some of them

travel-when you take more than 2 steps without dribbling the ball (you can do 3 steps with gather step which is legal)

double dribble- when you pick up the ball and start dribbling again

goaltending- when you block the ball that is in downward motion or inside the cylinder of the rim

shot clock violation- you have 24 seconds to shoot the ball if timer runs out and you still have the ball then it will be a violation

8 second violation- when you stay on your side of the ccourt for 8 seconds

there are a lot of rules in basketball and each organization has different rules
